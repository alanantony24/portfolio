TracknShare Fitness Tracker

TracknShare was built as part of my Mobile Development module with Android module. This app allows users to track their runs, view run statistics and data such as timing, calories burned, speed and the path on the map that the user ran. Users could also share their progress with the inbuilt social media feature and like posts.

Eventsly

Eventsly was built as part of my MAD with iOS. This app allows people of all ages to find out what they love to do by connecting them with different events in their neighbourhood. For people who prefer not to meet, there are virtual events as well. The app also features a map feature where users can view the nearby events as pins on a map.
